# Privacy Notice

**Reach Team**, based in Mexico, in compliance with the **Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties** (LFPDPPP), its Regulations, and the Privacy Notice Guidelines, hereby informs you of this Privacy Notice, which governs the processing of personal data collected through the use of the **Reach** service, powered by **ReachX** technology, including services such as **ReachZ Athenas** (cloud service), **ReachS3** (cloud storage), and **Reach Client** (downloading instances for Minecraft).

## 1. Identity and Address of the Data Controller
**Reach Team**, based in Mexico, is the data controller responsible for processing personal data collected through the use of Reach services. For the purposes of this Privacy Notice, the Data Controller can be contacted via email at **privacy@reachsdk.online**.

## 2. Personal Data Collected
To provide Reach services, we collect the following personal and technical device data:

- **Personal Data**:
  - Full name (optional, if provided).
  - Email address associated with the Microsoft account.
  - In-game username (Minecraft).
  - Username of the device's operating system.

- **Technical Device Data**:
  - Motherboard serial number.
  - Unique device identifier (Device ID).
  - IP address.
  - List of applications open on the device at the time of using the service.

This data is collected to ensure the service's functionality, authenticate users, manage subscriptions, prevent fraud, and, where applicable, apply sanctions for non-compliance with usage policies through the **ReachX Anticheat** system.

## 3. Purposes of Data Processing
The collected personal and technical data is used for the following purposes:

### Primary Purposes:
- Create, manage, and authenticate user accounts for Reach services.
- Process payments and manage monthly subscriptions.
- Enable the creation and management of organizations (up to 5 per leader, depending on the subscription level).
- Facilitate the distribution of Minecraft instances via **Reach Client** and redeem codes.
- Operate the **ReachX Anticheat** system to detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized behavior.
- Provide technical support and customer service.
- Manage access for administrators and guest users invited by organization leaders.

### Secondary Purposes:
- Send notifications about updates, events, or promotions related to Reach (you may opt out of receiving these communications).
- Conduct statistical analysis and improve services.
- Evaluate service quality through optional surveys.

If you do not wish your personal data to be processed for secondary purposes, you may express this by sending an email to **privacy@reachsdk.online**.

## 4. Data Transfers
**Reach Team** does not transfer personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:
- To game studios hosting Minecraft events, for the management of instances and the application of sanctions in case of violations (e.g., misuse of beta or testing instances).
- When required by a competent authority in compliance with applicable legislation.
- To payment service providers to process subscriptions, who are obligated to comply with the same security and confidentiality measures.

In cases where data is transferred to game studios, they will act as data controllers for their own purposes, such as applying sanctions or managing events.

## 5. Security Measures
**Reach Team** implements technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect personal data, including:
- Encryption of sensitive data (e.g., motherboard serial number and Device ID).
- Restricted access to databases.
- Firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
- Internal confidentiality policies for staff.

In the event of a security breach, **Reach Team** will notify affected users and the **National Institute for Transparency, Access to Information, and Personal Data Protection (INAI)**, as required by applicable law.

## 6. ARCO Rights
You have the right to **Access**, **Rectify**, **Cancel**, or **Oppose** (ARCO Rights) the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please submit a written request to **privacy@reachsdk.online**, including:
- Full name and email address associated with your account.
- Clear description of the right you wish to exercise.
- Document proving your identity (optional, except in cases of rectification or cancellation).

**Reach Team** will respond to your request within a maximum period of 20 business days, in accordance with the LFPDPPP.

## 7. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies
The **Reach** service uses cookies and tracking technologies to enhance the user experience, authenticate sessions, and collect technical data. You may configure your browser to disable cookies, but this may limit some service functionalities.

## 8. Sanctions and ReachX Anticheat System
The technical data collected (e.g., motherboard serial number, Device ID, and IP address) is used by the **ReachX Anticheat** system to detect fraudulent behavior. In case of violations:
- The offending account may be suspended by the relevant game studio.
- In cases of severe violations, device data may be recorded to prevent the use of additional accounts on the same device.
- Unauthorized disclosure of beta or testing instances may result in permanent exclusion from testing events or legal action by the game studio, in accordance with their policies.

**Reach Team** does not apply global sanctions or manage the policies of game studios.
